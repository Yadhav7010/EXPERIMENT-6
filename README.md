# Synthesis of Graphene Oxide Nanosheets by Modified Hummers Method

## Aim
To synthesize graphene oxide (GO) nanosheets by the modified Hummers method.

---

## Materials Required

### Chemicals
- Graphite flakes  
- Sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄)  
- Phosphoric acid (H₃PO₄)  
- Potassium permanganate (KMnO₄)  
- Hydrogen peroxide (H₂O₂)  
- Hydrochloric acid (HCl)  
- Ethanol  
- Double distilled water  

### Apparatus
- Beaker  
- Conical flask  
- Measuring cylinder  
- Magnetic stirrer with hot plate  
- Thermometer  
- Ice bath setup  
- Filter paper  

---

## Formula

\[
W = N \times e \times l
\]

Where:
- **N** = Normality  
- **e** = Equivalent weight  
- **l** = Volume (in liters)  

---

## Procedure

1. **Preparation of Acid Mixture**  
   Mix sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄) and phosphoric acid (H₃PO₄) in a 9:1 volume ratio.

2. **Addition of Graphite**  
   Add 3 g of graphite flakes into the acid mixture and stir well.

3. **Oxidation Process**  
   Under constant stirring at 40–50°C, slowly add potassium permanganate (KMnO₄) to the mixture.

4. **Stirring**  
   Continue stirring the mixture for 24 hours while maintaining the same temperature.

5. **Termination of Reaction**  
   Add 400 ml of ice along with 6 ml of hydrogen peroxide (H₂O₂) to stop the reaction. The solution turns yellow/brown.

6. **Washing Process**  
   Collect the precipitate and wash repeatedly with:
   - Double distilled water  
   - 30% hydrochloric acid (HCl)  
   - Ethanol  

7. **Drying**  
   Dry the final product at 60°C to obtain graphene oxide nanosheets.
